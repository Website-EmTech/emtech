CLIMATE CHANGE IN
BAGUIO CITY

According to the World Wide Fund for Nature-Philippines, Baguio City is the most vulnerable to climate change among the four cities outside the National Capital Region because of its susceptibility to extreme weather conditions. The study found that Baguio City, the nation's summer capital, is most vulnerable to the consequences of climate change, with rain being its worst adversary. Intense storms might cut off Baguio City's economic activity due to floods and landslides.
